Health Benefits of Ginger

Ginger aids the stomach primarily by speeding up gastric emptying (moving food from the stomach to the small intestine), which reduces nausea, bloating, and indigestion. Its active compounds, gingerols and shogaols, soothe the gut lining, reduce inflammation, and calm cramping.

Key Benefits

  • Nausea Relief: Highly effective for morning sickness and motion sickness.
  • Improved Digestion: It increases motility, helping food pass quickly.
  • Reduces Bloating and Gas: Acts as a carminative to reduce intestinal gas.
  • Soothes Inflammation: Compounds act as antioxidants in the gut.
  • Lowers Acid Reflux: Decreases pressure on the lower esophageal sphincter.
